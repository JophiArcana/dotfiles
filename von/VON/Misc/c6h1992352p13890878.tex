Minimize $(xy+2)^2+(x-1)^2+(y-1)^2$ over real numbers $x$ and $y$.

---

The answer is $4$. Rewrite the expression as \[(xy+1)^2+(x+y-1)^2+4\ge4.\]
with equality achieved when $xy=-1$ and $x+y=1$.

---
